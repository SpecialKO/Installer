Uses Dege's excellent 32-bit D3D9 API implementation of dgVoodoo 2 to translate the game to Direct3D 11 and promote it to modern flip presentation model.

dgVoodoo 2.81.3 is provided as dgVoodoo.dll and proxy loaded by Special K alongside the TSFix plugin.
