Uses Dege's excellent 32-bit D3D9 API implementation of dgVoodoo 2 to translate the game to Direct3D 11 and promote it to modern flip presentation model.

dgVoodoo v 2.81.3 is provided as dgVoodoo.dll and proxy loaded by Special K alongside the TSFix plugin.

http://dege.fw.hu/ - https://github.com/dege-diosg/dgVoodoo2
